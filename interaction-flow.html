UseCase

Emily has been hearing about the video game Elden Ring and wants to learn more about it. She wants to find someone who is currently playing the game in order to see more of it. Emily decides she wants to find a new streamer who is playing it.

Precondition:

Emily is signed into Twitch on the home page.

Post Condition:

Emily follows a new streamer who is playing Elden Ring.

Interaction Flow

  • Emily clicks the search bar and types, "Eld"
  • Twitch returns some results for "Eld"
  • Emily continues typing "Elden Ring"
  • Twitch shows results for "Elden Ring" and related results
  • Emily clicks the search button
  • Twitch redirects to a result's page showing several streams currently playing "Elden Ring"
  • Emily clicks the first stream result by a streamer named Gladd
  • Twitch loads the stream page
  • Emily clicks on the follow button under the stream window
  • Twitch adds Gladd to her following list
